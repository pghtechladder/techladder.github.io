Support

Make an impact through giving


We launched our nonprofit coding bootcamp acknowledging an increasing demand for skilled, hard working software developers.  With the support of employer partners and community leaders we've been able to offer access to hands-on technical training but we need your help to ensure Delaware continues to be a thriving tech sector. 

Donate to Tech Ladder.  By supporting our program, you're investing in the lives of future technologists and helping to create a more diverse industry.  

How You Can Support 

Your donation helps provide accessible technology training to aspiring software developers and data engineers.

 
Please make a donation online with your debit or credit card. Tech Ladder, Inc. is a 501(c)(3). Contributions are tax deductible. Please consider adding an additional 3% to your donation to cover credit card processing fees (e.g., $25 + $0.75 = $25.75; $50 + $1.50 = $51.50; $100 + $3 = $103).

To make a donation through United Way, use code 19885.

Mail a check to: 
Tech Ladder
1007 N. Orange Street, 4th Floor
Wilmington, DE 19801

Volunteer your time. Building strong connections throughout the Delaware community and beyond is key to advancing Tech Ladder's mission. You can assist in this effort by volunteering your time to assist students with mock interviews, tutoring, mentorship, resume writing or speak at our "Meet the Experts" series. 

For more information, contact Lossie Freeman, Director of Corporate Partnerships.
