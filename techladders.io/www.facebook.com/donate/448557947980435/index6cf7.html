About
We urgently need your help now.

Today, Russia intentionally targeted and destroyed Ukraine’s largest Children’s Hospital and Oncology Center, Okhmatdyt, located in Kyiv. Many other hospitals and civilian infrastructure in Kyiv, Dnipro, Kryvyi Rih, Sloviansk, Pokrovsk, and Kramatorsk were also targeted in this large-scale attack, 37 have perished in the attack, with over 140 injured.

Currently in Okhmatdyt, children and medical personnel remain trapped under the rubble, and the rescue operation is ongoing. The largest pediatric facility where children receive oncological treatment and life-saving care is now destroyed....

Please support our emergency response efforts and help save Ukrainian children deliberately targeted by Russian attacks.

United Help Ukraine’s team is already on the ground, delivering much-needed medical and other supplies.

We will continue to keep you updated. See more
