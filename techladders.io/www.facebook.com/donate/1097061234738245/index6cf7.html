About
2024: Here we go, back for another year! Raising money for Pancreatic Cancer Action Network (PanCAN), which leads the world in the fight against pancreatic cancer.

Pancreatic Cancer is still the biggest adversary I face. We certainly have made progress, but we need help.

What progress, you ask?...

1. Robotic surgery - Most pancreatic surgery resections I do are now performed using the DaVinci Robot System, allowing for smaller incisions, better outcomes, faster recovery. (By the way, robotic surgery is not quite what is sounds like...There is no actual robot or artificial intelligence. It is just a highly sophisticated system of intricate instruments and tools controlled by the surgeon).

2.New treatments - including new types of chemotherapy, new aids for nutrition and wellness, even new concepts of of how to fight cancer.

3. New people - we need to keep training our newcomers to be the best they can be. Nutrition, therapy, young researchers

So how can you help?

You can donate money.

PanCAN will do the rest. If you want to join our team you can come on to the pier, but what I really need is for you to do is to.... wait for it.... wait for it.... wait for it....

DONATE MONEY.

There it is. I said it. If we don't ask, we aren't going to impact this disease. We need to raise money.

Thank you for reading this.
Thank you for caring.
Thank you for donating.

Nick Nissen, MD See more
