NFL Sunday Ticket Content Availability

You will generally receive out-of-market Sunday afternoon games unless the game is locally broadcast. A regular season Sunday afternoon home game could also be blacked out within the home territory of the home team if the game fails to sell out. Your billing address (zip code) is associated with your streaming access. However, due to NFL rules, you might also see blackout restrictions based on your mobile device's location (e.g., subscribers might not be able to stream while attending an NFL game or surrounding stadium areas). If there is an outage in your location, we'll do our best to let you know.
