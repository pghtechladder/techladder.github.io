Frequently asked questions

See answers to the most commonly asked questions about Promotions on YouTube.

Why can’t I access this feature yet?

Video promotions are currently available for all channels which meet conditions for Advanced features on YT and have >1k subs. Learn more on Advanced features here.

Will buying promotions guarantee me more views/subscribers for my channel?

Advertising is just one of many tools creators can leverage for audience growth and engagement. Visit here for more best practices on promoting your channel.

How is this different from Google Ads?

To enable an easier ads buying experience, creators are able to create simple ad campaigns for their videos within the new Promotions tab without being redirected to Google Ads. If you need more ad formats or advanced targeting options, consider creating your campaign in Google Ads.

What kind of ads can I buy?

Only in-feed video ads are supported, in-stream ads are not supported. Users need to engage with the ad in order to reach the watch page or subscribe.
