Become a data-first company with Tharav

Tharav is a SaaS-based Data Fabric and Data Mesh platform built by TechLadder that allows you to seamlessly access, integrate, model, analyze, provision, and monetize your data in no time at all, and at a fraction of the cost youd expect.

Organizations spend an incredible amount of time and money on data projects, only to get business outcomes from a mere 20% of them. Those arent very good odds, and Tharav aims to give your organization a better way to use the power of data for business results and improved efficiency.

How Tharav works

Data Fabric

Tharav uses this emerging data management design to provide a foundation of integrated data across various pipelines and cloud environments without physical movement, and the processes required to simplify access and facilitate self-service at scale.

Data Monetization

We instantly package data to multiple endpoints such as any BI tools, APIs, AI/ML platforms, and web apps to generate revenue or value for your organization and to define and establish control over data access.

Data Mesh

We use this decentralized format of data management to ensure that data consumers can access and analyze data according to their business domain or function without reliance on a data engineer.

What Tharav can do

It constructs virtual data sets from multiple sources and structures without physically moving any data.

It visualizes and analyzes virtual data sets using best-in-breed analytics tools and provides a low code/no code self-service approach to building data sets.

It creates a highly secured infrastructure and enables you to sell thoughtfully governed data products to consumers.

It places business, product, and data owners in the drivers seat.
