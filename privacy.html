Privacy Policy

 

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Tech Ladder, (which may be collectively referred to as, “we”, “us” and “our”). The following privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this website, www.techladder.io (referred to as our “site”).
It will notify you of the following:

       • What personally identifiable information is collected from you through this website, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.
       • What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.
       • The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information.
       • How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

 Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on our site. We have access to collect information that you voluntarily give us via email, online application or contact form.

We will use your information to respond to you regarding general questions or questions related to the application process. We will not sell or share your information with any third party outside of our organization.

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about events,  new classes, or news related to Tech Ladder.

Certain additional information is also collected from users of this website in an aggregate form and used solely in connection with improving the performance and responsiveness of our website. We may use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, or other technologies to collect certain information about visitors and users of our site. This information includes a user’s originating domain, time of visit, and domain name. Cookies do not extract personal data about you, such as your name or e-mail address. We use this information to monitor and improve our site, personalize your online experience, and for internal analysis.

Most internet browsers permit a user to accept, decline or delete cookies. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level but if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our websites.


Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may opt out of any future correspondence from us at any time. You can do so by emailing info@techladder.io, calling the phone number given on our website or by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of our emails.



Security

We take precautions to protect your information from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure or alteration. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline. Employees authorized to access Personal Data of others are required to take the appropriate safeguards to protect the Personal Data. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

 

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately by calling 302-256-5203 or emailing info@techladder.io.
