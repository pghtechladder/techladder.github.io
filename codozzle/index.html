Codozzle is an exciting initiative by TechLadder inclined towards the student community to motivate and help them to showcase their coding craftsmanship. On one hand, our mission is to improve and develop our clients’ business through cutting-edge technology solutions. About Us

What is Codozzle?

Well, Codozzle is a busy and intelligent community made up of student coders across colleges who come together to collaborate, upskill, solve problems and learn from renowned industry experts. Along the way, you will share insights, clear doubts, build your knowledge and tackle challenges; real-world problems from real businesses that need a creative mindset and exemplary skills to solve. Up for grabs are exciting prizes, recognition of your abilities by peers and experts, and a chance to work with TechLadder.
